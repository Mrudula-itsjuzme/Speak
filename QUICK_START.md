If you see rate limit errors (429), the app will automatically try other models:

3. `anthropic/claude-3.5-sonnet` (paid)
4. `openai/gpt-4o-mini` (paid)
5. `meta-llama/llama-3.1-8b-instruct:free` (free fallback)
